What is PPC

To start off our PPC explanation, let’s look at what PPC marketing is and how it works? PPC is a digital marketing strategy that involves displaying paid ads at the top of Google search results. These ads look like organic search results except for the “Ad” label in the top corner.

6 reasons why PPC is important

· PPC reaches a wide audience

· PPC is incredibly cost-effective

· PPC can reach the right people

· PPC drives fast results

· PPC lets you measure results

· PPC is easily testable and alterable
